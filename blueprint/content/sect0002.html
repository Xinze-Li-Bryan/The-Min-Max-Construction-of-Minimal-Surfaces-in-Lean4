2 Basic Definitions

Definition 2.1 Closed Riemannian 3-Manifold

MinimalSurfaces.ClosedRiemannian3Manifold A ✓ closed Riemannian 3-manifold is a compact metric space of dimension 3.

Definition 2.2 Embedded Surface

MinimalSurfaces.EmbeddedSurface ✓def:closed_riemannian_3_manifold An embedded surface in a manifold \(M\).

Definition 2.3 Area Functional

MinimalSurfaces.area The area of an embedded surface.

Definition 2.4 Minimal Surface

MinimalSurfaces.is_minimal A surface is minimal if its mean curvature vanishes.
