3 Varifold Theory

Definition 3.1 Varifold

MinimalSurfaces.Varifold A \(k\)-varifold in an \(n\)-dimensional manifold is a Radon measure on the Grassmann bundle.

Definition 3.2 Integer Varifold

MinimalSurfaces.IntegerVarifold An integer varifold is a varifold with an associated multiplicity function.
